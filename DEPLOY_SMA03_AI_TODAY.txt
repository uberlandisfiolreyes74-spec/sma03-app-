TECHNICAL DETAILS:
─────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────

Formula implemented:
├─ II(t) = [1 - ∏(1 - Pi * A_avg * B_avg * FD)] * D_t
├─ P = average of P_E, P_R, P_L, P_S, P_M
├─ A_avg = average of C, I, S, A_V
├─ B_avg = average of B_R, B_E, B_C
├─ FD = triggering factor (user input)
└─ D_t = e^(-0.001*t) where t = days since decision

Calculations:
├─ Real-time updates (< 10ms)
├─ 13 input sliders (0-1 range)
├─ 3 output visualizations
